The Republic of Liberterra

Citizenship

To apply for citizenship, go to the National Citizenship Office in Le Center-Ville, Liberterra

Visas

Every visitor of the Republic of Liberterra must have a visa, UNLESS the visitor is a citizen of Qatar. Citizens of The Republic of India who were born on or after 26 January, 1950 can apply for an e-visa. CITIZENS OF THE PEOPLE'S REPUBLIC OF CHINA, THE REPUBLIC OF KOREA, THE PEOPLE'S REPUBLIC OF KOREA, THE ISLAMIC REPUBLIC OF IRAN, AND THE EUROPEAN UNION ARE ALL STRICTLY BANNED FROM ENTERING THE REPUBLIC OF LIBERTERRA. ANY PERSONS WITH SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19 ARE STRICTLY BANNED FROM ENTERING THE REPUBLIC OF LIBERTERRA. To apply for a visa to The Republic of Liberterra, please email visas@yopmail.com
